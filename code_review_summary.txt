The final solution addresses three layers of the reported ComboBox issue:
1. Hit-testing: MainWindow's window-drag logic was intercepting PointerPressed events before they reached the ComboBox. Added a visual tree traversal to ignore interactive controls.
2. Overlay Interference: The OverlayHost (used for animations) was potentially blocking hit-tests even when transparent. Added IsVisible toggling to ensure it's completely inactive when not animating.
3. Styling/Theming: Ensured the Dark theme is active and that ComboBox/ComboBoxItem components have appropriate colors for visibility.

The solution is robust and adheres to Avalonia's visual tree principles.
